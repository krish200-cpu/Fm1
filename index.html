STRATEGIC DESIGN SPECIFICATION ### 1.1 Intro Slogan Redesign **Objective:** Transition user from chaotic/stressful external context to a focused, calm digital environment. * **Option A (Selected for Production):** *"Dignity in every detail."* * **Rationale:** Shifts focus from "death" (the problem) to "dignity" (the outcome). Validates the user's desire to do the right thing. Short enough to read in 1.5s animation window. * **Option B:** *"The trusted path forward."* * **Rationale:** Emphasises guidance and movement. Reduces the feeling of paralysis common in grief. * **Option C:** *"Clear choices. Compassionate care."* * **Rationale:** Functional and descriptive. Good for A/B testing against emotionally driven slogans if conversion rates are low. ### 1.2 Primary Action Architecture (The "Triad of Need") **UX Principle:** Do not force a user in crisis to read generic copy. Segment immediately by urgency. | Action Category | Label Copy | Microcopy | Button Text | Interaction Outcome | | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | | **Immediate** (Crisis) | "A death has occurred" | "We can arrange collection from home or hospital immediately." | "Request Support" | **Modal Overlay** (No page load latency). Direct dispatch form. | | **Standard** (Search) | "Compare Directors" | "View pricing, reviews, and availability for independent directors." | "Start Search" | **Redirect** to `/search`. Standard funnel entry. | | **Future** (Planning) | "Plan Ahead" | "Secure peace of mind by freezing costs at today's prices." | "Explore Plans" | **Redirect** to `/plans`. Educational lead-in. | ### 1.3 Interaction & Journey Enhancement **Soft-Touch Mechanics:** 1. **The "Curtain Raise":** The site does not "load" abruptly; it fades in. This mimics the biological response of "adjusting one's eyes," reducing cognitive shock. 2. **Hover Deceleration:** Buttons do not snap; they breathe. CSS transitions set to `0.4s cubic-bezier` to mimic physical weight, implying reliability. 3. **Non-Destructive Modals:** The "Immediate Help" modal must be dismissible easily (click outside) to prevent feeling trapped, but sticky enough to prevent accidental closure. --- ## 2.
